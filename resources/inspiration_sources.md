## Collection SOP / 灵感采集流程

1. **Build categorized collections**: Character Ref / Motion Ref / Scene Ref / Audio Trends — maintain 20+ unused items per category
2. **Weekly refresh**: Spend 30 minutes per week replenishing your library
3. **Track platform trends**: TikTok Creative Center / Reels Audio Library for trending sounds
4. **Tag everything**: Use consistent naming and tagging for quick retrieval
