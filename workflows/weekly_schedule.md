# Weekly Production Schedule / 每周节奏模板

## Overview

A repeatable weekly rhythm that produces 14-21 pieces of content in ~3 hours of active work.

---

## Weekly Calendar

### Monday — Inspiration & Research (30 min)

- [ ] Browse trend sources for 15 min:
  - TikTok Creative Center (trending sounds)
  - Instagram Reels Audio Library
  - Top-performing content in your niche
- [ ] Select 10 character references from Pinterest
- [ ] Select 10 motion references from Pinterest
- [ ] Save 5 trending audio tracks
- [ ] Note 3-5 caption angles that are working
- [ ] Update your inspiration folders

### Tuesday — Batch Creation (60-90 min)

**Block 1: Images (30 min)**
- [ ] Generate 10 base character images
- [ ] Generate 10 before/after transformation pairs
- [ ] Quality check all 20+ images

**Block 2: Videos (30-60 min)**
- [ ] Submit 10 video generation jobs
- [ ] While waiting: write captions and select audios
- [ ] Download and review all generated videos
- [ ] Re-generate any that don't pass quality check

### Wednesday — Editing & Scheduling (60 min)

**Block 1: Edit (40 min)**
- [ ] Open CapCut template
- [ ] Edit all 10 videos:
  - Import video + audio
  - Add text overlay
  - Sync cuts to beat
  - Verify 10-15s duration
- [ ] Export all: 1080x1920, H.264, 30fps

**Block 2: Schedule (20 min)**
- [ ] Upload to scheduling tool (Later/Buffer/native)
- [ ] Set posting times:
  - 3 posts/day × 7 days = 21 posts
  - Rotate: TikTok → Reels → Shorts
- [ ] Double-check captions and hashtags

### Thursday-Sunday — Monitor & Engage

- [ ] Check analytics daily (5 min)
- [ ] Respond to comments (5 min)
- [ ] Flag any breakout performers for remake
- [ ] Start collecting references for next week

---

## Posting Cadence

```
Mon:  TikTok 8AM  |  Reels 12PM  |  Shorts 7PM
Tue:  TikTok 9AM  |  Reels 1PM   |  Shorts 8PM
Wed:  TikTok 8AM  |  Reels 12PM  |  Shorts 7PM
Thu:  TikTok 9AM  |  Reels 1PM   |  Shorts 8PM
Fri:  TikTok 8AM  |  Reels 12PM  |  Shorts 7PM
Sat:  TikTok 10AM |  Reels 2PM   |  Shorts 9PM
Sun:  TikTok 10AM |  Reels 2PM   |  Shorts 9PM
```

Adjust times based on your audience analytics after 2-3 weeks.
